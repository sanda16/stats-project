Purpose of the suvey:
The annual agriculture (farming) and related services collects data on the nature (activities) and structure of the agriculture (farming) and related services industry. The collected information is use by government for policy formulation, decision making and to monitor performance of the industry. These statistics can also be used in analyses of comparative business and investment decisions

Collection authority: The information required is collected under section 16 of the Statistics Act, 1999 (Act No.6 of 1999). Your cooperation is sought in completing and submitting this questionnaire by the due date. According to the Act, the provision of the information is sought compulsory.
